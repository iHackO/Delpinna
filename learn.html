Dolphins are at the top of the food chain which means that they help evenly stabilize the marine environment. With an endangered population of a few thousand, these species of dolphins keep on declining and the need for your help keeps on inclining. Just to name a few - Maui's dolphins, Baji dolphins, and vaquita dolphins, which all combined are roughly 600 brilliant yet helpless mammals.

Solutions that may help combat this phenomonon is to...

  • Decrease polluting and littering practices.
  • Eliminate usage of dolphines for entertainment such as aquariums and shows.
  • Participate and donate to organizations like WWF | WorldWildLife.org
  • Join in and sign petitions to ban netting
